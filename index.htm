Why Jotted? x

You can use Jotted to write down quick notes or scribble something within the browser without the need to open any application.

Entered text is temporarily stored in the browser's memory and does not transfer or stored to the server. You can retrieve your text even after closing and reopen the browser or tab and be navigating to https://joted.github.io/.

Click Save button on top-right corner of the page to save your text on your local hard drive.

Please provide your feedback and comment on Joted GitHub page.
